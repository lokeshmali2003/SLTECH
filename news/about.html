MEET THE FOUNDER

"Every itinerary we build starts with one question — what would make this traveler want to come back?"

Saransh Sharma started Saransh Holidays & Forex out of a single desk in Jaipur in 2013, after years of leading pilgrimage groups through Uttarakhand himself. That hands-on experience on the road — knowing which mountain passes close early, which guesthouses actually deliver, and how to keep a Char Dham group calm and on schedule — still shapes how every package is planned today. Under his leadership, the company has grown from a regional Char Dham specialist into a full holidays-and-forex operation serving over 18,000 travelers across 45+ destinations.

OUR JOURNEY

Over a decade of getting travelers there

From a single desk in Jaipur to a full holidays-and-forex operation, here's how we grew alongside the travelers we serve.

2013

Saransh Holidays begins operations in Jaipur, focused on Uttarakhand pilgrimage tours.

2016

Char Dham Yatra packages launch, with dedicated on-ground coordinators along the route.

2019

Forex services added, letting international travelers arrange currency through the same desk.

2022

International packages introduced, covering Dubai, Thailand, Bali and Singapore.

2025

Crosses 18,000 travelers served, with 45+ destinations now on offer.
